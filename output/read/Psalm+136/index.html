Psalm 136

Oh give thanks unto Jehovah; for he is good; For his lovingkindness for ever. Oh give thanks unto the God of gods; For his lovingkindness for ever. Oh give thanks unto the Lord of lords; For his lovingkindness for ever: To him who alone doeth great wonders; For his lovingkindness for ever: To him that by understanding made the heavens; For his lovingkindness for ever: To him that spread forth the earth above the waters; For his lovingkindness for ever: To him that made great lights; For his lovingkindness for ever: The sun to rule by day; For his lovingkindness for ever; The moon and stars to rule by night; For his lovingkindness for ever: To him that smote Egypt in their first-born; For his lovingkindness for ever; And brought out Israel from among them; For his lovingkindness for ever; With a strong hand, and with an outstretched arm; For his lovingkindness for ever: To him that divided the Red Sea in sunder; For his lovingkindness for ever; And made Israel to pass through the midst of it; For his lovingkindness for ever; But overthrew Pharaoh and his host in the Red Sea; For his lovingkindness for ever: To him that led his people through the wilderness; For his lovingkindness for ever: To him that smote great kings; For his lovingkindness for ever; And slew famous kings; For his lovingkindness for ever: Sihon king of the Amorites; For his lovingkindness for ever; And Og king of Bashan; For his lovingkindness for ever; And gave their land for a heritage; For his lovingkindness for ever; Even a heritage unto Israel his servant; For his lovingkindness for ever: Who remembered us in our low estate; For his lovingkindness for ever; And hath delivered us from our adversaries; For his lovingkindness for ever: Who giveth food to all flesh; For his lovingkindness for ever. Oh give thanks unto the God of heaven; For his lovingkindness for ever.
